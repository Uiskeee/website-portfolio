CakeCakeCake!

2D Party Game for PlayStation 5

Overview

CakeCakeCake! is a 2D party game where players play as the characters Strawberry, Tiramisu, Lemon or Cookies & Cream and partake in a series of minigames where winning means getting a cake! Players must race to stack 3 cakes in order to win.
CakeCakeCake! draws inspiration from Mario Party for its gameplay and Paper Mario for its cute, papery artstyle.


It was made for my final year project over a span of 3 months, in a team of 5, including 2 artists.

My Role

  • Project Manager: Assigned work and managed communication between artists and developers.
  • Lead Gameplay Programmer: Developed the game's core systems and gameplay

Technical Challenges

The biggest challenge was shaping together a functional framework that would fit our needs for the game.
